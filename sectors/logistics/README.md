# Logistics

Companies operating in delivery, fleet management, ride-hailing, freight, and last-mile transport in Tanzania.

## What to Expect in Interviews

- **Real-time location** and dispatch algorithms.
- **Route optimization** under constraints (traffic, fuel, driver hours).
- **Event-driven architectures** — rider/driver/order state transitions.
- **Push notifications and WebSockets** for live tracking.
- Handling **GPS noise** and low-signal areas.

## Adding a Company

1. Copy `_template/` to a new folder named after the company (lowercase, hyphenated).
2. Fill in `stack.json` — must validate against the root `schema.json`.
3. Fill in `interview-questions.md` following the template structure.
4. Add at least one source to `stack.json` (see [contribution-standards.md](../../docs/contribution-standards.md)).
