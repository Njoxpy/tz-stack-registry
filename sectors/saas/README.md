# SaaS

B2B software platforms, developer tooling, and vertical SaaS companies operating from or targeting Tanzania.

## What to Expect in Interviews

- **Multi-tenancy** design (schema-per-tenant vs. row-level, billing, isolation).
- **REST and GraphQL API** design, versioning, and rate limiting.
- **Authentication and authorization** at scale (RBAC, SSO, audit logs).
- **Background jobs** and workflow orchestration.
- **Observability** — logging, metrics, tracing as first-class concerns.

## Adding a Company

1. Copy `_template/` to a new folder named after the company (lowercase, hyphenated).
2. Fill in `stack.json` — must validate against the root `schema.json`.
3. Fill in `interview-questions.md` following the template structure.
4. Add at least one source to `stack.json` (see [contribution-standards.md](../../docs/contribution-standards.md)).
